## Basic Navigation

`?` - Show help

`tab` - Switch between sections
`j + k` - Scroll up and down
`s` - Start/stop scanning
`q` - Quit

`p` - Pair the device
`d` - Enable discovery
`space` - Connect to device
`t` - Trust/Untrust device
